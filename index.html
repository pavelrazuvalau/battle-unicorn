About

Battle Unicorn is a shooter game based on fandom Fallout Equestria universe. There are constantly spawning enemies and your goal is to kill as much as possible. Initially you have a pistol. Get a higher score by killing enemies to unlock better weapon.

Controls

Movement: W A S D

Shoot: Left mouse click

Reload: R

Weapon choose: 1 2 3

Pause: ESC
